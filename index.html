About

How I got here

Avital Tzubeli started her career as a software engineer. Then she got on a stage, and everything changed.

She discovered that the tech leaders who could tell a great story were the ones who moved rooms, closed deals, and built followings. The ones who couldn't, didn't.

That insight became the mission. Over the past five years, Avital has trained top executives, senior engineers, and product teams at companies like Google, Amazon, and Melio to communicate with the clarity, confidence, and presence their ideas deserve. She also teaches teams how to integrate AI workflows into their daily work, because the teams that learn the tools communicate even smarter.

She's a public speaker, a workshop facilitator, and a DJ, because she believes there are many ways to captivate a room. All of them require the same thing: knowing your audience, owning your presence, and giving people a reason to pay attention.

She's based in New York City, and she's probably already thinking about how to make your next corporate conference unforgettable.
